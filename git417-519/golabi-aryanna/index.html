Welcome to Niche!

Niche is a place for people to explore, learn and be inspired by plants. The motivation was simple, to make choosing the right houseplant easier. Since opening our doors in 2013, in Boston's South End, we have been focused on bringing plants into people's lives, and giving our customers the proper information on how to care for them.

Indoor Cacti

Cactus (Cacti) will steal your heart with their charming, prickly forms and showy flowers. They require some caution when handling, but these super low maintenance plants can thrive indoors with bright sun, little water, and proper drainage. As the stunning products of harsh, arid climates, cacti are both the pinnacle of succulent perfection for enthusiasts and easy-to-grow starter plants for beginners.
