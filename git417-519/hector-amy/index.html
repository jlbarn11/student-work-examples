About

In 2019, Casey Webb started an Esty shop to share her unique jewelry designs with the world. What started out as a small side-gig became her passion. After devoting all her free time to Gold Rush for two years, her business expanded to a four-woman team that ships internationally. Her mission remains the same, but Gold Rush Jewelry has flourished into a thriving boutique operated in the Pacific Northwest.

Still women run, we focus on offering one-of-a-kind pieces with your style in mind. By working closely with our designers, we offer styles inspired by classic aesthetics, but refined for the modern day with trends and pop culture in mind. We ethically source stones and materials to create unique fine and fashion jewelry. We're your one stop shop for exceptional quality and design.
