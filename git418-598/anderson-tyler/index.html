Why choose Ajax?

Training Center

From bodybuilding to powerlifting and everything in between, Ajax Fitness has the equipment to help you reach your goals. With dumbells up to 150lbs, 7 squat racks, 5 deadlift platforms, 2 competition benches, and a variety of plate loaded and pin loaded machines, we have everything you need.

Guidance

Whether you are just starting out, or have been training for awhile, you'll have a great community supporting you every step of the way. Our team of Personal Trainers and Nutritionists are available to answer any questions you may have.

Access Anywhere

We are with you wherever you travel! Download our app to get access to videos and training plans wherever your journey takes you.

Featured Classes

Yoga

This class incorporates yoga postures, gentle movement sequences, breath work, supported silent meditation, and guided relaxation to support increased awareness and mindfulness of the breath and body, and quieting of the nervous system.

Weightlifting

This class is designed to give clients the opportunity to learn weight training concepts and techniques used for obtaining optimal physical fitness. Clients will benefit from comprehensive weight training and cardiorespiratory endurance activities.

Boxing

This class incorporates 60-minute sessions where we will cover intermediate boxing technique and strategy, as well as the conditioning and strength training necessary to excel in the ring. You will encounter complex drills and combinations.
